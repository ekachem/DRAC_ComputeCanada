## Digital Research Alliance of Canada (DRAC)

The [Digital Research Alliance of Canada](https://www.alliancecan.ca/en) (formerly `Compute Canada`) is a national non-profit organization funded by the Government of Canada. Its mission is to provide researchers across all academic disciplines with a sustainable, researcher-centric, and integrated **Digital Research Infrastructure (DRI)** ecosystem.
